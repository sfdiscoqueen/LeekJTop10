5. Improve Sleep Health

Most adults need between 6 and 9 hours of sleep every night, but according to a recent survey, the average American gets just six hours and 19 minutes sleep a night. Keeping to a schedule will help your body settle into a routine, supporting your sleep-wake circadian rhythm for more restful and restorative sleep.
